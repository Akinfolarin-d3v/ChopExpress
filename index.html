Our Story

Every Flavor Tells a Story

Discover Delight in every bite at ChopExpress, your go-to destination for authentic West African flavors! Founded in Ghana, we're passionate about serving international and traditional dishes made with locally sourced ingredients and time-honored recipes. From savory samosas, crispy chicken drumsticks, and flavorful kelewele, every meal at ChopExpress brings the rich taste and culture of West Africa right to your table. Whether you're dining in or on the go, we're here to share the comfort and warmth of top-notch cuisine with you!
